How to Download Answer Key?

How to Download JEE Advanced 2020 Answer Keys?

Given below are the steps to access JEE Advanced Response Sheet:

  1. Visit the official website i.e. jeeadv.ac.in.
  2. Click on - “Response Sheets”.
  3. Login using Registration Number, Date of Birth, Mobile Number and Email ID.
  4. Response Sheet will appear.
  5. Download and save the answer key in the PDF format for future reference.
Steps to Challenge Answer Key

How to Challenge JEE Advanced 2020 Answer Key?

If there are any discrepancies, candidates can challenge them by sharing their feedbacks at feedback.jeeadv.ac.in/. The link will be activated after publishing of answer keys. For every question to be reviewed, processing of INR 500 is charged which can be paid online. The official answer keys are referred for evaluating JEE Advanced scores. Based on official answer keys, the result will be declared.
