Bachelor of Science in Airlines, Tourism and Hospitality Management Top Colleges, Syllabus, Jobs, Salary

B.Sc. in Airlines, Tourism and Hospitality Management is a 3-year undergraduate course, involving an advanced study of the aviation, tourism, and hospitality management industry.

Aviation, tourism, and hospitality businesses involve areas of operation such as customer support, operations, or general administrative job roles.

Some of the institutes that offer the course in India are listed here:

  • Sai Nath University, Ranchi
  • Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar
  • Chandigarh University, Chandigarh

Some of the top entrance tests conducted for admission to the course in India are:

  • AIEEE: All India Engineering Entrance Examination.
  • AICET: All India Common Entrance Test.
  • JNU: Engineering Entrance Exam.

The basic eligibility criterion for pursuing the course is a 10+2 or equivalent qualification in the Science stream, from a recognized educational Board, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

Admission to the course is based on the candidate’s performance in a relevant entrance exam, or merit obtained in the last qualifying exam.

The average tuition fee charged for the course in India ranges between INR 10,000 to 5 Lacs for a span of 4 years.

Successful graduates of the course can expect anything between INR 3 to 20 lacs as annual salary in the industry, increasing with experience and skillset.

Such graduates are hired in capacities such as:

  • Customer Care Officer, Front Office, Receptionist, Operations Manager.
  • Tour Operator, Assistant Sales Manager, Information Assistant.
  • Lobby Manager, Operations Executive, Reservations Executive, Sales Manager.
  • Ticketing Executive, Professor & Lecturer, Accounts Assistant.
  • Hotel Management Trainee, Executive Facility Management.
  • Guest Tourism Manager, Travel and Tourism Manager, Relationship Manager.
  • Senior Executive/ Assistant Manager, Tour Management Executive
  • Travel Consultant, etc.

B.Sc. in Airlines, Tourism and Hospitality Management: What is it About?

The components of study primarily covered within tis course’s curriculum include aviation management, tourism & hospitality management.

Successful graduates of the course are gainfully employed across a range of areas, such as avionics, tourism, and hospitality management.

Students enrolled to the course are imparted advanced learning in international tourism, and awareness of the dynamic nature of tourism in modern society.

B.Sc. in Airlines, Tourism and Hospitality Management: Who should opt?

Ideal candidates for the course would possess the following:

  • An understanding of products, structures, and operations within the tourism industry.
  • An insight into the nature and characteristics of tourists.
  • Good communication and presentation skills, which are essential in an industry where customers are of central importance.
  • A friendly personality, ability to handle people, verbal fluency, and aptitude for languages.

Most institutes offering the course admit students based on performance in a relevant entrance test, often followed by a round of personal interview, wherein their general aptitude for the course is tested. Admission process generally varies across colleges.

B.Sc. in Airlines, Tourism and Hospitality Management: Career Prospects

In the government sector, such professionals are hired across Government Tourist Information Offices, Tourism Boards, Airlines, Government-run Hotels, Transportation, etc.

In the private sector, such professionals may work for Travel agencies, Travel Consultancies, Airports, Tour operators, Airlines, Travel and ticketing websites, Hotels, Visa and travel document tourism firms, Resorts, Cruise lines and Tourist information office, etc.
