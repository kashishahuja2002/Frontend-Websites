JEE Advanced 2020 Application Form and Exam Date

JEE Advanced Registration is a completely online process. The application fee can be paid in online mode using Debit/Credit Card/Net banking or in offline mode at SBI branches. Check out the following table for important dates related to JEE Advanced Registration.

Event Date
Commencement of registration September 11, 2020
Last date of registration September 16, 2020
Last Date to Pay Application Fee September 17, 2020
JEE Advanced 2020 September 27, 2020

JEE Advanced 2020 Admit Card

Candidates who register for the exam can download their admit cards from the official website. The admit card will be released on September 21, 2020. The admit card must have the following information of the candidate on it-

  • Name of the Candidate
  • Roll Number of JEE Advanced 2020
  • Photograph
  • Signature
  • Date of Birth
  • Category
  • Address of Correspondence
  • Date of Birth
  • Category
  • Address of Correspondence
  • Name & Address of the Examination Centre

      • Keep the admit card safe for Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT). The same JEE Advanced admit card will be used for AAT. Both the papers- Paper 1 and Paper 2 of the JEE Advanced will be conducted on the same day. Both of them are compulsory. The two papers will be of 3 hours each. The test will be conducted in English and Hindi. JEE Advanced Exam Pattern

JEE Advanced 2020 Result and Seat Allotment Dates

  • JEE Advanced 2020 Result will be declared around August after the exam. Text messages will also be sent to all the candidates on their registered mobile numbers.
  • Qualifying candidates will be allocated seats in various IITs, NITs, etc. by Joint Seat Allocation Authority.
  • The schedule of counselling will be announced separately by JoSAA 2020 after the result is declared.
  • Only candidates who appeared for both Paper 1 and Paper 2 will be ranked and considered for counselling.

Result, Counselling and Seat Allotment dates are elucidated in the following table:

Event Date
Release of Provisional Answer Key September 29, 2020
Challenge JEE Advanced Answer Key September 29–30,2020
JEE Advanced 2020 Final Answer Key October 5, 2020
Declaration of Result October 5, 2020

Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT) 2020

Candidates who want to join the architecture program available only at IIT Kharagpur and IIT Delhi will have to appear for an Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT) 2020. Considering the delay in JEE Advanced events, AAT’s revised dates shall be declared soon.

  • Only candidates who qualify JEE Advanced can appear for the AAT.
  • Candidates must register for the test on the official website www.jeeadv.ac.in
  • The test will be conducted only in English.
  • Admit card and Result for the test will be available on the JEE Advanced official website.
  • Seats will be allotted based on the All India Ranks of the candidates.
