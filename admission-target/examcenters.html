  • Dubai (UAE)

JEE Advanced Exam Center 2020 – Important Instructions

  • JEE Advanced admit card must be presented for verification in the exam center.
  • Material to be brought to the exam center: Admit card, ballpoint pen with transparent body, pencil, eraser and original photo identity card.
  • Report to the exam centre before 07:45 Indian Standard Time (IST) and cooperate with the staff for physical frisking and biometric verification.
  • The exam will start at 09:00 IST in the forenoon session and 14:00 IST in the afternoon session. Candidates will NOT be allowed to enter the exam center the after commencement of exam in both the sessions.
  • No candidate will be allowed to leave the exam center before the completion of the examination.
  • Mobile phones, bluetooth devices, earphones, microphones, paper, health band or any other electronic gadgets, any printed/blank/hand written paper, log table, writing pad, scale, geometry / pencil box, pouch, calculator, pen drives, electronic pen, wallet, handbag, goggles or similar such things are NOT allowed inside the exam centre.
