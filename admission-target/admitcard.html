How to Download JEE Advanced Admit Card?

Steps involved in downloading JEE Advanced admit card are indicated below:

  1. Go to the official website- www.jeeadv.ac.in
  2. JEE Advanced Admit Card is available on candidates’ portal.
  3. Enter Registration Number, Date of Birth, Email ID and Phone Number.
  4. Enter Registration Number, Date of Birth, Email ID and Phone Number.
  5. Download JEE Advanced Admit Card and take its print out.
Note: A copy of the downloaded JEE Advanced admit card should be produced at the time of online exam. The original JEE Advanced 2020 admit card will be issued to the candidate during the Paper 1 of the JEE Advanced examination. This original admit card must be retained safely till all the formalities of admission are complete.

Details Mentioned in JEE Advanced Admit Card

The details mentioned in JEE Advanced 2020 admit card are as follows:

  • Candidate’s name
  • Roll number for JEE Advanced 2020
  • Parent’s/Guardian’s Name
  • Question Paper Language
  • Photograph & Signature of the candidate
  • PwD Status
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Aadhar Number
  • IIT Zone
  • Language mode of question paper
  • Venue & Code of exam center
  • Exam Schedule
  • Exam Day Instructions

Importance of JEE Advanced Admit Card

  1. Admit card is the admission ticket to the JEE Advanced exam. It is mandatory for the verification purpose. Without it, candidates will not be allowed to enter the exam hall.
  2. It is checked at the entrance of the exam hall along with a proper identity card. On proper identity verification, these candidates will be given original admit cards.

Discrepancy in JEE Advanced Admit Card

Candidates should clearly check the details mentioned in JEE Advanced Admit Card, in case of any discrepancy, contact the Chairman, JEE Advanced 2020 of the respective zonal coordinating IIT. They may face difficulty to access admit cards for JEE Advanced 2020 because of the following reasons:

  • Server Error :At times when there is severe online traffic, the online portal gets down or stops responding which results in candidate’s inability to download admit cards. Once, traffic gets cleared and the server starts responding candidates can download their admit card. It should be noted that clearance of heavy online traffic is quite lengthy and may take a day or two.
  • Providing Wrong Information : While downloading the admit card, if candidate happens to enter wrong details such as incorrect registration number or the date of birth, he/she will not be able to download the same. Thus, in order to avoid such situation, aspirants should make sure that they enter correct details.
  • Partial Filling of Application Form : Aspirants must satisfy all the details stated in the registration form while filling it up. They must check the form again before submitting. Incomplete information and failure to pay examination fee will result in the cancellation of the application procedure. Hence, no admit card will be issued to the respective student.

Documents to be Carried to Examination Center

The documents are key to access to enter the examination center. Those who fail to carry the below-mentioned documents will not be allowed to sit for JEE Advanced exam.

  • Original photo identity proof of the applicant
  • JEE Advanced 2020 Admit Card
Note: Carry your own pens and pencils. Any e-device, log table, paper, wallet are not allowed to carry.

Forgot Registration Number?

IIT Delhi will enable the facility to access the forgotten registration number for JEE Advanced 2020. Steps to retrieve JEE Advanced Registration Number are:

  1. Visit the official portal of JEE Advanced 2020.
  2. Click on link for JEE Advanced Admit Card.
  3. Click on Forgot Registration Number.
  4. Enter JEE Main 2020 Roll Number and Date of Birth in DD-MM-YYYY format.
  5. Submit the details and save the registration number.

JEE Advanced Exam Day Guidelines

  1. The JEE Advanced admit card must be presented for verification in the exam center. Carefully check your Admit Card. In case of any discrepancy, contact the Chairman. JEE (Advanced) 2020.
  2. Material to be brought to the examination hall: Admit card, ballpoint pen with a transparent body, pencil, eraser, and original photo identity card.
  3. Report at the examination center before 07:45 Indian Standard Time (IST) and cooperate with the staff for physical frisking and biometric verification.
  4. You will NOT be allowed to enter/leave the exam center during the examination in both the sessions.
  5. Mobile phones, bluetooth devices, earphones, microphones, paper, health band or any other electronic gadgets, any printed/blank/handwritten paper, log table, writing pad, scale, geometry / pencil box, pouch, calculator, pen drives, electronic pen, wallet, handbag, goggles or similar such things are NOT allowed inside the examination centre.
  6. It is advisable NOT to wear charms, veil, items containing metals such as ring, bracelet, earrings, nose pin, chain/necklace, pendants. badge, brooch, hairpin, hair band, clothes with full sleeves or big buttons. It is advisable to wear open footwear like chappals and sandals.
  7. Violation of any of the rules and regulations at any stage of the examination will lead to the cancellation of the candidature.
